About

NIRMAAN is a platform for Civil engineers to hone their technical and managerial skills. This very society is steering their growth but it is not confined to the academic premises only, the managerial and the technical skills are in tandem with academics. Being a campaign focussed at promotion of futuristic and eco-friendly technologies or technical innovations aimed at eradicating problems faced by the common people, NIRMAAN intends to grow into a huge tree with young civil engineers as its branches extending technological support and solutions in developing our national infrastructure.We are making a bridge that will connect the imaginations of students to real engineering.

Nirmaan believes that each of us must find some meaning in our work. You do your best when you know that it's not just work, but something that will improve other people's lives. We inspire to imagine because imagination is the impetus behind knowledge. Knowledge is limited, imagination knows no borders. Though NIRMAAN is new in AKGEC, we know that "The road to success is always under construction!”

A Platform for civil engineers to have a voice, loud and clear!
