About Us

Robotics Club AKGEC is a open source community where we work upon amazing projects like Soft Robotics, 3D Printer, Quadcopter, etc. Also participate in national as well as international competitions like Robocon and WRO.

Robotics Club represents Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College in various National and International competitions like: ABU Robocon, Mitsubishi Electric Cup, World Robot Olympiad, NI-Yantra, Technovation, Smart India Hackathon.

The technologies that we use in our robots to perform any perticular tasks are : Cad Designing, Image Processing, Embedded Systems, Pneumatics, Sensors, Electronics,
