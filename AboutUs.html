About Us

TechMania is the information based website of Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College which brings to its students the illumination of the knowledge glow of various societies and labs present in the College.
The Website "TechMania" established in 2020.


'TechMania' allows access to the whole information about the societies present in the college. We provides a virtual tour of all the societies. Here we provides the latest information about societies. This website is designed for assisting the students of the institute regarding all the knowledge required by them before joining any society or giving preference to the one over the other.
